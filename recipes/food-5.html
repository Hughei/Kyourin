  • Sakura Mochi

    Demon Slayer

    Sakura mochi, which consists of sweet pink rice with the patented red bean paste filling that's wrapped up in a cherry blossom leaf. Mitsuri Kanjori from Demon Slayer takes her obsession with this dessert to new heights. Apparently, the reason her hair is pink is that she eats a ton of sakura mochi, which is just the most ridiculous fact ever.
      Ingredients:
    • • 1 ½ - 2 cups water
    • • Red food coloring
    • • 300 g mochigome sweet rice
    • • 1 cup red bean paste
      Directions:
    • 1. Thoroughly wash your rice.
    • 2. Mix water with 1-2 drops of food coloring.
    • 3. Mix your mochigome with the colored water in a rice cooker and cook.
    • 4. Stir up your mix to make sure it's combined properly and fill with additional water as needed. (The water should come above the rice level.)
    • 5. Let the rice soak for about sixty minutes and then cook.
    • 6. Mash your cooked rice into paste and let it cool down.
    • 7. Split your red bean paste into 12 rolled balls.
    • 8. Slowly begin taking 1/4 cup of rice and slowly rolling them each scoop around your red bean paste balls.
    • 9. Use plastic wrap to seal and form an oval shape with your ball.
