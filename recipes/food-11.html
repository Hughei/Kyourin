  • Mitarashi dango

    Demon Slayer

    Mitarashi dango are traditional Japanese rice flour dumplings that are skewered onto sticks in groups of 3–5 and are covered with a sweet soy sauce glaze. It is characterized by its glassy glaze and burnt fragrance.
      Ingredients:
    • Wrapper:
    • • 1 cup Rice flour/joshinko
    • • 1 cup Rice flour/shiratamako
    • • Water
    •  
    • Mitarashi sauce
    • • 2 teaspoon Mirin
    • • Water
    • • 4 teaspoon Potato starch
    • • 4 teaspoon Sugar
      Directions:
    • Dango
    • 1. Add a 1 cup of joshinko
    • 2. 1 cup of shiratamako
    • 3. Add a water base in your like consistency
    • 4. Mix it and set it aside
    • 5. And cook in boiling water
    •  
    • Mitarashi sauce
    • 1. Toss the mirin
    • 2. Add soy sauce
    • 3. And water, and after the liquid are combined
    • 4. And lastly mix the potato starch and sugar before tossing in
    • 5. Mix it well in the medium heat
